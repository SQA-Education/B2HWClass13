This homework only practices Class 13 content: job qualifications, experience, key interview vocabulary, and using past and future tenses to answer interview questions.

1. Reading

Preparing for an Interview

Marcos is preparing for an interview for a receptionist position. He has a degree in hospitality and two years of experience working at a hotel. Last week, he updated his resume and contacted two references.

During the interview, he plans to explain his qualifications, skillset, and training. He will also talk about a customer-service certification he earned last year. He hopes the interviewer will see that his experience matches the company’s needs.

Marcos is also going to prepare answers about why the company should hire him. He believes his communication skills and professional attitude will help him do well.
